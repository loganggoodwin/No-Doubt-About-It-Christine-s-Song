About the Song

No Doubt About It — Christine’s Song is a heartfelt country rock family tribute using computer infrastructure and cybersecurity language as metaphors for love, protection, connection, and commitment.

The song honors Christine Goodwin, the family Garth and Christine built together, and the truth that family is not defined only by a last name. It is defined by love, loyalty, protection, and the bonds that stay connected through every season of life.
