# No Doubt About It — Christine’s Song

**From Garth to Christine Goodwin**

## Verse 1
Christine, from the day I took your hand  
July twenty-fourth, nineteen ninety-eight  
We started building more than dreams  
We built a love that time can’t break  

Through every storm and every mile  
You were my signal, strong and true  
My home network, my safest place  
My heart always routes back to you  

## Pre-Chorus
We patched the cracks, we stood our ground  
Kept our uptime when life went down  
Through every error, every test  
You were the system I loved best  

## Chorus
No doubt about it, you’re my heart and my light  
My firewall in the middle of the night  
You’re my backup plan, my power supply  
The reason I keep reaching high  

No doubt about it, this love is real  
Stronger than steel, stronger than shield  
Christine, you’re my forever route  
No doubt about it, no doubt about it  

## Verse 2
Two beautiful girls, Kate and Halie  
Three strong boys, Dakota, Xavier, Samuel too  
Different last names may tell different stories  
But they’re all family through and through  

Every child is loved the same  
Every heart has a special place  
Blood, names, and roads may differ  
But love is what keeps this family safe  

And now ten grandkids fill our days  
With laughter, stories, hugs, and grace  
Our family tree keeps branching out  
Like a network running all through the house  

## Pre-Chorus

You kept the best parts safe in me

## Bridge
We may not all carry the same last name  
But love never checked a login screen  
Family is more than what’s written down  
It’s every bond that lives between  

Same heart, same home, same endless love  
Same protection when life gets rough  
No matter the name, no matter the line  
You’re all connected to this heart of mine  

You’re my encryption when the world feels cold  
My access control when I need to hold  
You’re my backbone, my server rack  
My incident response when life attacks  

You’re my router, my switch, my trusted DNS  
My clean connection when life’s a mess  
Through every packet, every scan  
You helped me become a better man  

## Verse 3
From hard drives full of memories  
To photos saved from years gone by  
Every birthday, every Christmas morning  
Every tear you helped me dry  

We’ve had crashes, we’ve had restarts  
But never lost what matters most  
You’re the password to my heart  
The one I love, the one I chose
