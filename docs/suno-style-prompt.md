# Suno Style Prompt

## Full Style Prompt

Emotional country rock love ballad, heartfelt male vocals, warm acoustic guitar, soft piano, light drums, uplifting chorus, family tribute, modern inspirational sound, emotional bridge, clean production, sentimental but strong, about lifelong love, blended family, marriage, children, grandchildren, cybersecurity and computer infrastructure metaphors.

## Song Description / Theme

A heartfelt tribute song from Garth to his wife Christine Goodwin. The song celebrates their marriage on July 24, 1998, their two girls Kate and Halie, their three boys Dakota, Xavier, and Samuel, and their ten grandchildren. The song should express that even though the children and family members may have different last names, they are still one family, and every child is loved the same. Use computer infrastructure and cybersecurity terms as metaphors for love, protection, strength, loyalty, and family connection.
